Nick Cannon Takes Rumored Girlfriend Winnie Harlow on Lavish Date

Love after Mariah? Nick Cannon was spotted treating rumored girlfriend Winnie Harlow to a lavish dinner date in Beverly Hills on Tuesday, Sept. 22.

The America’s Got Talent host, 35, enjoyed a candlelit dinner at Mr Chow with Harlow, 21, a stunning model with the skin condition vitiligo.

For the occasion Cannon, who filed for divorce from Mariah Carey last December, wore a jazzy black and purple pin-striped suit with glittery silver loafers. Harlow, whose birth name is Chantelle Brown-Young, wore a burgundy mini dress with gladiator buckle heels. The two were photographed getting into a limo together. The young model has been hailed as a role model for those with vitiligo, a rare skin condition characterized by portions of skin losing their pigment.

Neither Cannon nor Harlow have spoken about their rumored relationship.
