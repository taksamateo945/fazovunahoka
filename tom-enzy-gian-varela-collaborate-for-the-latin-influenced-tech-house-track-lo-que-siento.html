Tom Enzy &amp; Gian Varela collaborate for the Latin-influenced tech-house track &quot;Lo Que Siento&quot;

Bringing tech-house wrapped around Latin-influenced energy, as you’ve never heard before, is Tom Enzy & Gian Varela with the awesome new single “Lo Que Siento” (which translates to “What I Feel”). This follows up Tom Enzy’s single “Flamenco” and Gian Varela’s collaboration with Damon Sharpe titled “Faces” featuring Matluck.

Tom Enzy, real name José António Fernandes Coelho, is a highly successful artist, having achieved multi-platinum sales with a string of hit releases on some of the industry’s biggest labels including Sony, Universal, Warner, Insomniac, Selected, and Spinnin’ Records. His music has amassed over 100 million streams on Spotify alone, making him the #1 streamed artist in Portugal for two consecutive years. Tom Enzy is widely recognized in the dance music world, having collaborated with global stars such as Alok, Rick Ross, Pitbull, and Bob Sinclar. He has performed at major music festivals including Tomorrowland, Ultra Music Festival, Secret Garden, and Viva Fest. He was also listed among the top 150 DJs in the world by DJ Mag in 2019. The recent years have been incredibly successful for Tom Enzy, with many multi-million streamed tracks and his co-written smash-hit “In The Dark” with Purple Disco Machine and Sophie & The Giants achieving incredible feats and charting worldwide. With numerous high-profile collaborations and releases in the pipeline, 2023 is poised to be Tom Enzy’s most significant year yet.

Gian Varela, the globally renowned DJ/producer and songwriter, was born in Panama and honed his craft in the USA. With a rebellious spirit, Gian Varela’s innovative approach to merging sounds and genres defines his unique artistry. His formative years in Panama, the birthplace of reggaetón’s roots, and his move to the USA have influenced his sound. From his viral collaborations with Latin superstars like Sech, Feid, and El Chombo, to releases on the world’s leading dance music labels such as Spinnin’ Records, Armada Music, Mixmash Records, and Revealed Recordings, as well as festival tracks and remixes with the support of luminaries such as Armin Van Buuren, Gianluca Vacchi, and Fedde Le Grand, few artists manage to prove themselves as much as Gian Varela does. With early performances across the Americas, Gian Varela has developed an impressive festival presence, captivating massive crowds in South America, electrifying clubs in Ibiza, and igniting afterparties in Ecuador with his “guerilla” style show. A master producer, Gian Varela can fluidly navigate between genres and this year, he plans to showcase what Latin dance music truly means. From a repertoire of sultry Spanish house tracks to more Latin urban pop tunes, his upcoming collaborations and high-energy fusions in the dance music world are sure to bring his signature global sound to the forefront.

Written and produced by the artists themselves, “Lo Que Siento” is a sexy sultry tech-house anthem that fuels Latin vibes into the mix linking effortlessly with the Spanish lyrics and addictive house melody. The driving force of the deep bassline connects the entire track together whilst the groovy-yet-ravey drop spins up the flow of the track. As the spoken word lyrics mesh into the rhythm of the dance track, it’s the repeated chorus that truly makes the impact, heating up the vibe with accompanied determined dance floor-ready beats that only merge with the repetitive line “De lo que siento”, making the spoken-word line into a beat itself, before the unstoppable groovy waves unleash effortlessly during the drop.
