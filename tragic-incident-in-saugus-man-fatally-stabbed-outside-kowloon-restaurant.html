Tragic Incident in Saugus: Man Fatally Stabbed Outside Kowloon Restaurant

In a tragic incident, a man has died after being stabbed outside the well-known Kowloon Restaurant on Route 1 in Saugus, Massachusetts. The incident occurred just after 9 p.m. on Saturday, according to local authorities.

The victim, whose identity has not yet been released, was found by police in the restaurant’s parking lot, suffering from a knife wound. Despite immediate medical attention, he succumbed to his injuries.

This is the second violent incident at the iconic Kowloon Restaurant in less than a month, raising concerns about safety in the area.

Essex County District Attorney Paul F. Tucker confirmed the fatality and stated that an investigation is underway. Authorities are currently working to determine the circumstances that led to the stabbing and are appealing to the public for any information related to the incident.

The tragic loss of life has left the community in shock, and our thoughts go out to the victim’s family and friends during this difficult time.
