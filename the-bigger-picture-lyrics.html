About

Lil Baby, whose real name is Dominique Armani Jones, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter from Atlanta, Georgia. He initially gained fame in 2017 with his mixtape “Perfect Timing” and has since released several successful projects, including his debut album “Harder Than Ever” in 2018 and the critically acclaimed “My Turn” in 2020.

Lil Baby’s music typically deals with themes of street life, poverty, and perseverance through difficult circumstances. He has collaborated with numerous other artists, including Drake, DaBaby, and Lil Durk, and has been nominated for multiple awards, including a Grammy for Best Rap Performance in 2021 for his song “The Bigger Picture,” which addresses police brutality and the Black Lives Matter movement.
