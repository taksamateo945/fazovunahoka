Couple Caught Having Sex on Red Carpet at Cannes Lions Festival

Now that's a public display of affection! A couple at the Cannes Lions International Festival of Creativity in southern France was caught having sex on the red carpet on Monday, June 22, as evidenced in a Twitter photo shared by one bystander.

"Mad respect for this couple having blatant sex on the #CannesLions red carpet just now," David Griner, the managing editor of digital for Adweek tweeted. Griner also gratuitously shared a faraway photo of the couple in the heated moment with one person straddling the other. Hilariously, an official festival poster hovered in the frame with the words, "Will see you in the morning!"
